Pet Policies for 33 West Avenue

Sr. No Policies
(click on policy to see details)
1
Pet Registration

  • All pets must be registered with the society management.
  • Pet owners must provide vaccination records and comply with local pet regulations.
  • Any new pet must be reported to the society office within 15 days of adoption or acquisition.
2
Pet Owner Responsibilities

  • Owners must ensure their pets do not cause inconvenience or harm to other residents.
  • Pets should be leashed while in common areas such as lifts and parkings and should not be left unattended.
  • Owners must ensure their pets relieve themselves outside the society premises.
3
Noise & Disturbance Control

  • Excessive barking or loud noises from pets that disturb other residents should be controlled.
  • In case of repeated complaints, owners must take corrective actions.
4
Use of Common Areas

  • Pets are not allowed in children’s play areas, swimming pools, terrace gardens or other restricted zones.
  • There are no designated pet-friendly zones in the society; thus, owners should take their pets outside for walks.
    •

5
Hygiene & Health Requirements

  • Pets must be regularly vaccinated and groomed to ensure hygiene and public safety.
  • In case of contagious illnesses, pets should be kept indoors until fully recovered.
6
Banned Pets & Breeds

  • Banned pets or breeds, as per local regulations, are not allowed in the society.
  • Exotic or dangerous animals that pose a risk to residents are strictly prohibited.
7
Liability & Complaints

  • Owners will be held responsible for any damage caused by their pets to society property or individuals.
  • Any disputes or complaints regarding pets will be handled by the society committee.

Tenant Policies for 33 West Avenue

Sr. No Policies
(click on policy to see details)
1
Tenant Registration & Approval

  • All tenants must be registered with the society management.
  • The owner must inform the society management at least 15 days before a tenant moves in or out.
  • A valid rental agreement, government-approved ID, and police verification are mandatory for tenant approval.
  • Procedure for tenant:
  • Submit Rent Agreement, Police Verification, Government-approved ID and Move-In charges to the Property Manager.
  • Receive NOC / Gate Pass
  • Show Gate Pass at the Gate while moving in. Security has been informed not to allow any transport vehicle without Gate Pass, so please cooperate.
    •

2
Move-In & Move-Out Guidelines

  • Move-ins and move-outs should be scheduled during society-approved hours from 10 AM to 6 PM.
  • A move-in charge of Rs. 2000/- and move-out charge of Rs. 2000/- will be levied from the owner.
  • The tenant or owner must ensure that any shifting-related damages to society property are compensated.
  • A No Dues Certificate must be obtained from the society before vacating.
3
Security & Access

  • Entry and exit of tenants and their guests must comply with the society’s security protocols.
  • Tenants should inform security about visitors in advance when required.
  • Any suspicious activity should be reported to the security personnel immediately.
4
Maintenance & Society Fees

  • The owner remains responsible for all society maintenance charges, unless agreed otherwise in writing.
  • Any damage to common areas by the tenant will be chargeable to the respective unit owner.
5
Parking Regulations

  • Tenants should follow the driveways and must use only the designated parking space allotted to the flat.
  • Unauthorized parking in other slots or common areas will result in fines or towing at the tenant’s expense.
6
Usage of Common Facilities

  • Tenants can use society amenities like the gym, clubhouse, and swimming pool, subject to society rules.
  • Booking of community halls or other facilities must be done in advance and is subject to availability.
  • Any misuse of facilities may result in revocation of access.
7
Waste Management & Environmental Responsibility

  • Tenants must follow the society’s waste segregation and disposal guidelines.
  • Water and electricity should be used responsibly to avoid wastage.
8
Tenant Conduct & Compliance

  • Tenants must adhere to all society rules, including noise regulations, pet policies, move-in & move-out guidelines and common area usage.
  • Any disturbances, including loud music, parties, or illegal activities, will be subject to penalties.
  • Tenants must respect the privacy and peace of other residents.
  • Subletting the flat is not allowed in the society.
    •

9
Dispute Resolution

  • Any disputes between tenants and the society will be handled by the society’s committee.
10
Termination of Tenancy

  • The society reserves the right to request eviction if a tenant repeatedly violates policies.

Car Parking Policy for 33 West Avenue

Sr. No Policies
(click on policy to see details)
1
Parking Rules

  • Strictly no parking is allowed in common areas.
  • All residents are required to use only their designated parking slots.
  • Visitor parking must be used only by visitors and not by residents unless authorized.
2
Penalty for Unauthorized Parking

  • Owners occupying visitor parking or common areas without permission will attract a fine of Rs. 300 per day.
  • The penalty amount will be automatically added to the maintenance bill of the respective flat owner.
3
Vehicle Stickers and Entry Protocol

  • Cars must display valid society-issued stickers for direct entry through the main gate.
  • Vehicles without stickers, even those of owners, must make a manual entry at the gate.
    •
  • Residents are expected to cooperate with security guards and not argue if entry protocols are enforced.
4
Collection of Car Stickers

  • Residents must collect their car stickers from the society office.
  • Failure to do so may result in delays at the gate due to manual entry requirements.
5
Gate Waiting Policy

  • If any car is waiting at the entrance lobby gate for emergency purposes or onboarding/offloading, ensure:
  • The driver remains inside the car, and
  • The entrance is not occupied for more than 5 minutes.
  • Security is authorized to request movement if this is violated.
6
Security Cooperation

  • Residents are expected to cooperate with security staff.
  • Any disputes or arguments at the gate will be reported to the management committee.
