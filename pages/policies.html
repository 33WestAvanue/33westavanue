Pet Policies for 33 West Avenue

Sr. No Policies
(click on policy to see details)
1
Pet Registration

  • All pets must be registered with the society management.
  • Pet owners must provide vaccination records and comply with local pet regulations.
  • Any new pet must be reported to the society office within 15 days of adoption or acquisition.
2
Pet Owner Responsibilities

  • Owners must ensure their pets do not cause inconvenience or harm to other residents.
  • Pets should be leashed while in common areas such as lifts and parkings and should not be left unattended.
  • Owners must ensure their pets relieve themselves outside the society premises.
3
Noise & Disturbance Control

  • Excessive barking or loud noises from pets that disturb other residents should be controlled.
  • In case of repeated complaints, owners must take corrective actions.
4
Use of Common Areas

  • Pets are not allowed in children’s play areas, swimming pools, terrace gardens or other restricted zones.
  • There are no designated pet-friendly zones in the society; thus, owners should take their pets outside for walks.
    •

5
Hygiene & Health Requirements

  • Pets must be regularly vaccinated and groomed to ensure hygiene and public safety.
  • In case of contagious illnesses, pets should be kept indoors until fully recovered.
6
Banned Pets & Breeds

  • Banned pets or breeds, as per local regulations, are not allowed in the society.
  • Exotic or dangerous animals that pose a risk to residents are strictly prohibited.
7
Liability & Complaints

  • Owners will be held responsible for any damage caused by their pets to society property or individuals.
  • Any disputes or complaints regarding pets will be handled by the society committee.
